Consumer Responsibilities

Your Responsibilities as a Consumer

Total consumer protection is a collective effort, its actualization demands collective input from not just manufacturers, service providers and Government, but also from the consumer. The consumer has the responsibility to:

Beware: be alert to the quality and safety of goods and services before you purchase.

Be Aware: gather all the information and facts available about a product or service as well as keep abreast of changes and innovations in the market.

Think Independently: make decisions about well- considered needs and wants Speak Out: inform manufacturers and Government of your needs and expectation

Complain: inform business and other consumers in a fair and honest manner about your dissatisfaction and satisfaction with a product or service

Be an Ethical Consumer: be fair and never engage in dishonest practices, which costs all consumer money

Respect Environment: avoid waste ,littering and contributing to pollution. It is hoped that as consumers become aware of their rights and responsibility, and report any unremedied infringement to the Council, the dynamics of a free market economy will be triggered off to generate a better sustainable, thriving and growing economy in the nation. Such would remove wasteful expenditures, reduce poverty as value for money gets assured.
